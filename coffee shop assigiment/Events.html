Open Mic Night



Host a night of music, poetry, or stand-up comedy performance by locals. Comically invite patrons to showcase their talents in a friendly atmosphere; free drink for performers. Promote on social media and embed a few from the past performances to get excitement going.

Latte Art Workshop



Offer hands-on workshops where your customers could learn the latte art. Guided by an expert Barista, the trainees will try their hand on making hearts and leaves among other latte art patterns. Showcase some nice pictures of pretty latte creations and the testimonials of participants to entice more attendees.

Tasting and Pairing of Coffee



Organize a tasting session with various coffee beans from around the world. Pair each type with complementary treats like pastries or cheeses, and explain the unique flavors. Share tasting notes and bean origins on social media to entice coffee enthusiasts.

Book Club Gathering



Start a monthly book club, selecting a new book each month and meeting to discuss it over coffee. Partner with a local bookstore for book discounts and post a reading list and discussion schedule. Capture book club highlights to encourage new members to join.
